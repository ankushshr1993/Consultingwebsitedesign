## The Context

India's defence and dual-use innovation ecosystem is moving from promise to execution.

Engineering capability is stronger, procurement pathways are becoming more credible, and international demand for reliable, cost-efficient security technology continues to expand.

What is still missing in many early companies is not ambition—it's institutional readiness for long-horizon capital.

- Decision-grade governance
- Practical export preparedness
- Cross-border operating design
- Credible exit pathways

This is where we focus our work.

## What We Believe

Technology attracts attention.
Structure attracts enduring capital.

In defence, execution quality is judged over years, not months. Startups that build institutional discipline early create compounding strategic and financial advantage.

## What We Do

We operate as an operator-led investment partner.

- Identify: We evaluate defence and dual-use startups with genuine scaling potential beyond domestic procurement cycles.
- Institutionalise: We help founders build governance rhythm, capital architecture, export awareness, and investor-grade operating clarity.
- Align Capital: We deploy personal capital first, then bring aligned institutional investors once readiness is visible and defensible.

## Our Focus

We prioritise opportunities with:

- Dual-use scalability
- Software and autonomy leverage
- Sensor-led system intelligence
- Durable unit economics
- Cross-border relevance
