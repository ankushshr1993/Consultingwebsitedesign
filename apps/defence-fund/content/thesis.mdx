## The Context

India's defence innovation ecosystem is entering a new phase of maturity.

Core technical capability is strengthening. Government pathways for validation and procurement are becoming clearer. At the same time, global demand for resilient, cost-efficient defence technology continues to rise.

Yet institutional capital remains under-deployed—not because innovation is absent, but because foundational structures are often not investor-ready.

- Governance quality
- Export realities
- Cross-border feasibility
- Exit design

This is where we operate.

## What We Believe

Capital does not follow technology first. It follows structure.

Defence startups rarely fail for lack of innovation alone; they underperform when institutional discipline arrives too late.

We believe early structural alignment creates measurable long-term value.

## What We Do

We operate as an operator-led investment partner.

### 1 — Identify

We selectively evaluate defence and dual-use startups with the potential to scale beyond domestic procurement cycles.

### 2 — Institutionalise

We work alongside founders to strengthen:

- Governance
- Capital structure
- Export-control awareness
- Institutional positioning
- Exit logic

### 3 — Align Capital

We invest personally and introduce institutional capital only when structural readiness is demonstrably in place.

We concentrate on depth, not deal volume.

## Our Focus

We prioritise technologies that demonstrate:

- Dual-use scalability
- Software and autonomy leverage
- Sensor-driven systems
- Margin resilience
- Cross-border applicability

We avoid capital-intensive, low-margin, grant-dependent models.
