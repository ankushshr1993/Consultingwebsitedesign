## Thesis Overview

Our thesis is built on one premise: in defence innovation, value is created when technical edge and institutional quality scale together.

## Why This Window Matters

India is producing increasingly credible defence technologies across software, autonomy, sensing, and mission-enabling infrastructure.

At the same time, global buyers are diversifying supply chains toward resilient, interoperable partners.

This creates a meaningful window for India-origin companies that can combine product excellence with procurement realism and export viability.

## How We Evaluate Opportunities

We test opportunities through four lenses:

- Mission relevance: does the solution solve a persistent, high-value security problem?
- Adoption pathway: can the company navigate procurement, validation, and deployment cycles?
- Economic quality: can margins and capital efficiency support durable growth?
- Strategic optionality: is there a credible path to international expansion and long-term liquidity?

## Portfolio Construction Discipline

We prefer concentration over volume.

Our approach is to support a limited number of teams where we can materially improve decision quality, fundraising readiness, and strategic positioning.

## What Success Looks Like

We seek companies that can become institution-ready strategic assets—businesses capable of compounding through strong execution, not narrative momentum.
